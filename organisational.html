Our Farm Units

Harvest & Sales Unit

This unit ensures every harvest is brought to market with care, from the field to your table.

  • Harvesting and packaging fresh produce
  • Managing direct-to-consumer sales and market stalls
  • Coordinating with local restaurants and retailers
  • Ensuring product quality and freshness

Farm Operations Unit

The Operations unit oversees the health of our fields, crops, and infrastructure.

  • Planting, cultivation, and soil management
  • Maintaining and repairing farm machinery
  • Managing irrigation systems and crop rotation
  • Monitoring for pests and plant diseases

Farmhand Management Unit

Our People & Culture team is focused on ensuring our team of farmhands is supported and thriving.

  • Hiring and training new farmhands
  • Ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions
  • Organizing team-building activities
  • Managing schedules and seasonal labor needs

Customer Relations Unit

This unit is our connection to the community, answering questions and building trust.

  • Handling inquiries from customers and partners
  • Collecting feedback to improve our products
  • Organizing farm tours and open house events
  • Managing our CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) program

Farm Finances Unit

This team manages our resources to ensure the sustainable and long-term health of our farm.

  • Managing budgets and cash flow
  • Handling invoices and payment processing
  • Applying for grants and agricultural loans
  • Tracking expenses and revenue

Logistics & Distribution Unit

This unit ensures our produce gets from our farm to its destination efficiently and in perfect condition.

  • Coordinating transportation and deliveries
  • Managing cold storage and warehouse operations
  • Optimizing delivery routes
  • Ensuring compliance with food safety regulations
