About GreenField Farms

A tradition of quality and sustainability.

Our Story

GreenField Farms was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: to cultivate healthy food in a way that respects the environment. For over three decades, our family has been dedicated to organic farming, believing that the best food comes from healthy soil and a balanced ecosystem.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our community with the freshest, most nutritious produce possible while acting as responsible stewards of the land. We are committed to sustainable practices, biodiversity, and fostering a connection between people and the food they eat.

Frequently Asked Questions

What farming methods do you use?

We are committed to sustainable and organic farming practices. We avoid synthetic pesticides and fertilizers, focusing instead on natural methods like crop rotation, composting, and integrated pest management to ensure healthy crops and soil.

Can I visit the farm?

Yes, we love visitors! We host open farm days and educational tours on a regular basis. Please check our website's "Events" section or contact us directly to schedule a visit.

Do you offer a CSA program?

Absolutely! Our Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program is a great way to enjoy fresh, seasonal produce. Members receive a weekly box of our farm's best offerings. Visit our "CSA" page for more details and sign-up information.
