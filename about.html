About GreenField Farms

A tradition of quality and sustainability.

Our Story

GreenField Farms was founded in 1985 by James and Eleanor Davis with a simple mission: to cultivate healthy food in a way that respects the environment. We've been a family-run operation from the very beginning, dedicated to a heritage of organic farming. We believe that the most flavorful and nutritious food comes directly from healthy soil and a thriving, balanced ecosystem.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our community with the freshest, most nutritious produce possible while acting as responsible stewards of the land. We are committed to sustainable practices, fostering biodiversity, and strengthening the vital connection between people and the food they eat.

Our Commitment to the Land

We practice regenerative agriculture to build healthy, nutrient-rich soil. This isn't just about avoiding chemicals; it's about actively improving the land's health. We use techniques like cover cropping, no-till farming, and integrated pest management to reduce our environmental impact and create a thriving ecosystem on our farm. Our dedication to these methods ensures that every vegetable and fruit we grow is bursting with natural flavor.

From the moment a seed is planted to the day it's harvested, our focus is on quality and integrity. We believe that a healthy farm creates healthy food, and healthy food creates a healthier community.
